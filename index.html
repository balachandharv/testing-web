About Project Expo

The National Level Project Expo – Zenofest, organized by the Tech Titans Association, Department of Information Technology, PSR Engineering College, provides a vibrant platform for students to showcase their innovative ideas, technical projects, and creative solutions.

This expo encourages innovation, teamwork, and problem-solving while offering students the opportunity to present their work to faculty, peers, and industry experts. It bridges the gap between theory and practical application, preparing young minds for research, entrepreneurship, and real-world challenges.

🤹 Mudincha Kandupudi

Coordinator: A. Raveena

Role: Event Organizer

Description: “Mudincha Kandupudi” is a fun Tamil-to-English word game. The Game Master gives a Tamil word, participants must quickly say its correct English meaning. A test of knowledge, speed, and excitement.

Rules:

>>> Round: 1 <<<

  1. Each team has minimum 2 members.
  2. No mobile phones allowed.
  3. Game master gives the word in Tamil.
  4. Participants must say English meaning within 10 seconds.
  5. Correct = 10 points, wrong = 0.
  6. Highest points win. Tie → 3 bonus words.

🔗 Connection

Coordinator: S.Nathiramumtaj

Role: Event Organizer

>>> Round: 1 <<<

BGM Round: In this fun round, participants listen to short clips of background music (BGM) from movies or shows and must identify the source. Tests memory and pop culture knowledge.

>>> Round: 2 <<<

Look and Tell: Teams are shown pictures or sequences of images and must guess the related phrase, movie, or concept. Tests observation, creativity, and teamwork.

Rules:

  1. Only 2 members per team allowed.
  2. No mobile phones or external help during the event.
  3. Each round will have a time limit.
  4. Even partial clues can help. Don’t give up easily!
  5. Maintain decorum; no distracting others.
  6. Judges decide winners. No arguments.
